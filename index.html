Our Story

Where Every Meal Tells a Story

Founded in 2005 by Chef Arjun Sharma, Yummy Yummy was born from a simple dream — to bring the warmth of home-cooked Indian food to every table, elevated with fine-dining finesse and heart.

From our slow-simmered Dal Makhani to our tandoor-kissed Paneer Tikka, every dish is a celebration of India's rich culinary heritage, crafted with the finest seasonal ingredients.
